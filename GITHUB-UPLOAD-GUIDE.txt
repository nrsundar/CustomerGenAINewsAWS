EASY UPLOAD METHOD:
1. In your GitHub repository, click "Add file" → "Upload files"
2. Drag and drop all files OR click "choose your files"
3. Write commit message: "Initial upload of CustomerGenAINews monitoring system"
4. Click "Commit changes"
